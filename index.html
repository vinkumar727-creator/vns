Discover Kashi

The Spiritual Capital of India

Banaras, also known as Varanasi or Kashi, is one of the world's oldest living cities and the spiritual heart of India. For over 3,000 years, this sacred city has been a beacon of spirituality, knowledge, and cultural heritage.

Nestled on the banks of the holy Ganges, Banaras is where life and death, past and present, divine and mortal converge in a timeless dance. From the ethereal Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat to the ancient lanes echoing with temple bells, every moment in Banaras is a step closer to the eternal.
