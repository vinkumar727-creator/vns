Getting There

How to Reach Varanasi

By Air

Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (VNS)

22 km from city center. Well connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, and other major cities. Direct international flights from select countries.

Airport to City: Taxis, app-based cabs (‚Çπ400-600), Airport bus available

By Train

Varanasi Junction & Banaras

Major railway stations with excellent connectivity. Varanasi Junction (Cantt) is the main station. Many daily trains from all major Indian cities.

Popular Trains: Shatabdi Express, Rajdhani Express, Duronto Express, Vande Bharat

By Road

Bus & Private Vehicle

Well connected by NH2 and NH7. Regular buses from nearby cities. Private taxis and buses available from all major destinations.

Distances: Delhi (820km), Lucknow (320km), Allahabad (125km), Gaya (230km)
